CrystalsCollector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

here are four crystals below, by clicking on one you will add a specific ammount of points to your total score.

The way you win the game is by matching your total score to random numbers, the way you lose is if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is random, it's given to you once you click on it.

Every time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
